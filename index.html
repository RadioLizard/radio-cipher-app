Note: This application is designed to decode and encode text based messages. It will not accept numbers or special characters with the following exceptions: period (.), comma (,), semicolon (;) dash (-), question mark (?), exclamation point (!), apostrophe (') and quotation marks ("). Messages with numbers or other special characters will be rejected.
